ABOUT*

ME

Greetings! I'm Gia Hao, also recognized as Eric, a first-year student enrolled in the BIT program at Red River College in Winnipeg, Manitoba. My expertise lies in the dynamic fields of web and software development, where I excel in creating visually appealing websites and pioneering applications. As a student in the BIT program, I introduce a unique and contemporary approach to both realms. Whether you're in need of a sleek website or seeking assistance with cutting-edge software projects, I'm here to help. Let's collaborate to turn your digital ideas into reality!

What Inspires Me?

In my world, inspiration springs from diverse sources. I am captivated by the elegance of well-designed websites, the awe-inspiring beauty of nature encountered during my travels, and the artistry of CSS and JS animations. The narratives of exceptionally excellent individuals, especially the stories of talented young minds making their mark, never fail to ignite my passion for innovation.

What I Hope to Learn?

My current journey of learning is a thrilling odyssey. I'm delving into JavaScript frameworks to elevate my web development skills and unraveling the complexities of advanced C#. Connecting databases to my projects is a skill I'm eager to master, and I'm exploring the collaborative world of GitHub and Git. The realm of Unity beckons as I embark on a journey into game development. Beyond technical skills, I aspire to broaden my network, connecting with individuals outside my field.

What I'm Currently Doing

Balancing my academic commitments in the BIT program at Red River College, I invest my free time in self-learning. The focus now is on mastering JavaScript, with the aim of achieving proficiency by the end of 2023 and venturing into new territories in the following year. Weekends see me at the Outlet Mall, engaging in a part-time job that complements my learning journey.

What Interests Me?

Professionally, I'm intrigued by career promotions and the continual evolution of my skills. On a personal level, my self-taught journey is a source of constant joy and excitement. I derive immense satisfaction from tracking the progress I make over time. Networking is a crucial aspect of my life, and I actively seek meaningful connections. Outside the professional sphere, my interests span from automobiles to immersive gaming experiences.

How I Spend My Free Time

In moments of leisure, you'll find me engrossed in self-learning, exploring new concepts and technologies. Cooking is both a creative outlet and a sensory experience I cherish. Tending to my collection of plants brings a sense of calm and responsibility. As the evening descends, I unwind with gaming, finding joy in virtual adventures.

Embark on this journey of growth, learning, and exploration with me!
